P.O.K.E.D.

The Personal Overengineered Kinetically Extending Digit is a mechanical toy made out of laser-cut MDF.

This started as a personal exercise for learning how to design mechanical systems using OpenSCAD.

The SVG files for laser-cutting are available here. You can find a step-by-step guide for building it over on Instructables

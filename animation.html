animation

I like animation. So I wanted to make some. Turns out it's a difficult process, and I don't yet have the dedication needed to do it proper. But I tried to learn it, using the tools I had on hand.

Tools

  • Animation ruler made from disposable chopsticks and a piece of strip wood
  • A bunch of paper
  • 3-hole puncher
  • A DIY light-table made out of a box frame and some LED strips
  • OpenToonz

Exercises

  • Walk cycle

  • Character on a pogo stick

  • Character sneezing

  • Laughing

Ressources

  • Dong Chang's video tutorials for doing traditional animation in OpenToonz
  • The Animator's Survival Kit by Richard Williams
  • Alex Grigg's Animation for Everyone series on YouTube

July 2025

Scotaire, the PBeS Studio duo and I founded Marave Goyave, a game maker collective from the East of France. We had a small game jam where I made SUPERHOTROIDS, a version of Asteroids where time only moves when you do.

I'm still working on it regularly, tweaking things here and there, so maybe I'll push more "complete" version ?

We also took part in the BBQ Game Jam 2025 where we made Slushie Heroes in 24h
